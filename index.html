"Fakirnama" Book Launch

A New Tribute to a Literary Legend

We are thrilled to announce the successful launch of "Fakirnama," a special volume dedicated to the life and works of Fakir Mohan Senapati. The event was a wonderful gathering of scholars, writers, and patrons of Odia literature.

This collection delves deep into the nuances of his masterpieces, offering fresh perspectives and celebrating his enduring legacy. Explore glimpses from the event and learn more about this significant publication.
